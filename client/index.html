    Preferanse Rules

    Setup

    • 3 players, 32-card deck (7 through Ace in all 4 suits)
    • Each player gets 10 cards; 2 go face-down as the Talon

    Bidding

    • Starting left of dealer, players bid contracts or pass
    • Bids: 6–10 in each suit (♠ ♣ ♦ ♥), NS (No Suit / no trump), or Misère
    • Suit strength (weakest → strongest): ♠ ♣ ♦ ♥ NS
    • Bidding ends when 2 players pass after the highest bidder
    • If all 3 pass → Raspasovka (take as few tricks as possible)

    Talon & Discard

    • The winning bidder picks up the 2 talon cards (everyone sees them)
    • They may raise their bid, then discard 2 cards secretly

    Whisting

    • The other 2 players each decide: Whist (play against bidder) or Pass
    • If only 1 whisters: they choose to play Open (show their hand) or Closed

    Playing

    • 10 tricks are played; trump = bid suit (no trump for NS; Misère = no trump, bidder wins by taking 0 tricks)
    • Must follow suit if possible; otherwise play any card
    • Home = number of tricks taken

    Scoring

    • Bidder makes contract → earns points in Taškai column
    • Bidder fails → penalty (Bauda) added to their pool
    • Whisters earn Whist points for tricks taken
    • Raspasovka → each trick taken = penalty to that player's pool
    • Game ends when any player's Taškai total reaches the target

    Contract Values

    • 6 = 2 pts, 7 = 4 pts, 8 = 6 pts, 9 = 8 pts, 10 = 10 pts
    • Misère = 10 pts (bidder must take 0 tricks)
    • NS adds no extra value — same as suit bids at that level
